Outreach Subteam

Community outreach is one of the most important ways The Fighting Calculators spread the message of FIRST. Through outreach in the community, the team hopes to reveal the opportunities available in FIRST programs, inform the public about STEM education, and ultimately inspire youth to pursue a career in STEM. The Fighting Calculators are proud to say that the team has had over 20 outreach events in 2017 alone.

The outreach team is responsible for planning demonstrations with our robot. These outreach events include demonstrations at elementary and middle schools, a showcase of the robot at a fair (such as Woodbury Days and the Minnesota State Fair), a demonstration for a corporate sponsor, or a team volunteering event (Feed My Starving Children). Demonstrations happen all throughout the year.
